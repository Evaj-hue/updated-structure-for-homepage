Don't put all your chips on one horse. Spread your bets across multiple horses to increase your chances of winning.

A horse with high speed but low stamina may burn out before the finish line. Look for balanced stats.

Favorites don't always win. Sometimes betting on an underdog with decent stats can yield bigger payouts.
